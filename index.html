Welcome to The Executive Chamber Student Organization


Our organization is committed to fostering entrepreneurial excellence among young entrepreneurship as well as upcoming entrepreneurs. It provides a platform/community where entrepreneurs have a safe space to expand and accelerate their crafts and skills and better their respective crafts and skills and better their respective businesses. Ideas and skills will be shared as well as techniques and strategies will be bounced off to help entrepreneurs better the value of their business. As the TECSO team, we strive for transformation and greatness and we believe in the power of entrepreneurship in this era.
